This is the statistics page. This page aims to give you a visual look into the current environment around your plant in order to compare them to the ideal scenario of the plants. 
The buttons on the left allow you to switch between the statistics of: temperature, humidity, light, and moisture(soil moisture).The graph is shown in the middle of the page 
and you can click and drag in order to zoom in as well as right click in order to zoom in a spexific axis(x or y) or let the graph auto range again in order to see the full set 
of data again. It will also allow the option to save the graph as a picture on your computer (.png file).
